Radio Codes
10-Codes & Signals
All Shadow Construction crew members are required to use standard radio codes during active sessions. These codes keep comms fast, professional, and realistic. All transmissions should end with your unit ID.
💡
Format: State your unit ID first, then the code. Example: "Unit 4 to Dispatch — 10-20 is the intersection of Main and 5th."

Uniform Guide
Required Dress Code
All members must wear the correct uniform during official sessions. Using the wrong outfit or no outfit at all may result in being asked to leave the session until corrected. Uniform items are available in the Roblox catalog and linked in Discord.
✅
Uniform links are pinned in the #uniforms channel on Discord. Management can approve alternate items if an exact match is unavailable.
🦺 Site Worker Worker
Hard Hat: Yellow safety helmet
Vest: High-vis orange or yellow safety vest
Shirt: Plain white or grey shirt underneath
Pants: Dark work pants (black or navy)
Boots: Black or brown work boots
No accessories that obscure the vest. No hats other than the hard hat during sessions.
🚧 Traffic Control Worker
Hard Hat: Orange safety helmet
Vest: Bright orange reflective vest (traffic variant)
Shirt: Plain white shirt underneath
Pants: Dark work pants
Prop: Traffic flag or stop/slow paddle if available
Traffic Control must always be visually distinct from standard workers on site.
📋 Foreman Senior
Hard Hat: White hard hat (distinguishes from crew)
Vest: Yellow safety vest with stripes
Shirt: Shadow Construction branded shirt if available
Pants: Dark cargo or work pants
Clipboard: Clipboard prop recommended for immersion
White hard hat is required to visually identify you as Foreman to the crew.
🚛 Equipment Operator Sr. Worker
Hard Hat: Yellow or orange helmet
Vest: High-vis vest (any approved color)
Shirt: Dark work shirt or branded tee
Pants: Dark cargo pants
Gloves: Work glove accessory recommended
Operators must stay in uniform even when inside vehicles during sessions.
🪖 Project Manager Senior
Hard Hat: White hard hat with "PM" label if possible
Vest: Optional — vest or branded jacket
Shirt: Shadow Construction official shirt
Pants: Smart dark trousers or cargo pants
ID Badge: Name tag or badge prop if available
Project Managers represent the company image — dress to reflect that.
🪖 Safety Officer Worker
Hard Hat: Green hard hat (industry standard for safety)
Vest: Green or lime safety vest
Shirt: White or grey shirt underneath
Pants: Dark work pants
Prop: Clipboard or safety sign prop
Green hat and vest are key — they signal "safety" to all crew on the site.

PROMOTION RULES

Nomination Window
Promotions are reviewed every Sunday at the Management Briefing. You must be nominated by a Foreman or above to be considered.
No Self-Nomination
You cannot nominate yourself for promotion. Asking management for a promotion directly may disqualify you from that cycle.
Strike Freeze
If you have an active strike on your record, you are ineligible for promotion until the strike expires or is cleared by management.
Time-in-Rank
Minimum time-in-rank requirements (see table above) must be fully met. Exceptions can only be made by the CEO or Director.
Attendance Requirement
You must have attended at least 60% of sessions during your current rank period to be eligible for promotion review.
Conduct Standard
Promotions are based on attitude, leadership, teamwork, and quality RP — not just how long you've been in the group.
