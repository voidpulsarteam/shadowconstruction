// CODE OF CONDUCT

GROUP RULES

[01]
Respect All Members

Treat every crew member, civilian, and officer with respect in-game and in Discord. Harassment is an immediate strike.

[02]
Stay In Character

During active sessions, remain in character at all times. OOC chat belongs in designated channels only.

[03]
No Trolling or RDM

We are a serious RP group. Trolling, random deathmatch, and griefing sessions results in immediate removal.

[04]
Follow Chain of Command

Respect the rank structure. Issues escalate through proper channels — not public drama.

[05]
Attend Scheduled Sessions

Regular session attendance is required for active rank retention. Post in absence channel if you can't make it.

[06]
Wear the Uniform

All members must wear the approved Shadow Construction outfit during official sessions. Details in the wiki.

[07]
No Leaking or Raiding

Internal session plans and group documents are confidential. Leaking or coordinating raids = permanent ban.

[08]
Have Fun & RP Hard

Bring energy, creativity, and commitment to every session. That's what makes Shadow Construction stand out.
